Publications

Some of the research papers that I published with the support and blessings of my professors and friends.

  • Market Segmentation Analysis and Visualization using K-Mode Clustering Algorithm for E-Commerce Business


    The paper describes the BI tool and a decision process for market segmentation based on user behavior analysis and geographical information. PCA, followed by the application of the k-mode clustering algorithm, has been applied for segmentation.

    CIT, Journal of Computing and Information Technology.

    Read More

  • Quantitative analysis of historical data for prediction of job salary in India - A case study


    In this paper an attempt has been made to develop a quantitative approach for predicting the factors that affect the salary of an individual. The Aspiring Minds’ Employability Outcomes (AMEO-2015) dataset consisting of Aspiring Minds’ Computer Adaptive Test (AMCAT) score along with job seeker personal and employment details of Indian students has been considered for the study.

    Journal of Statistics and Management Systems

    Read more

  • An Implementation Based Comparative Analysis of Various Vulnerabilities in Online Forms


    In this paper an attempt has been made to implement SQL injection and spambots attacks using Selenium, jQuery and Python. The techniques used to detect and prevent this vulnerability are also discussed.Presented this paper in National Seminar - Techno Tryst 2017 , Digital Transformation : Computational and Technological Advancements held at Delhi Institute of Advanced Studies.

    Techno Tryst,2017
